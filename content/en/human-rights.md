Environmental Justice is fundamental to Immigrant Justice

Caucus Human Rights Committee advocates, assists and organizes regarding the needs of immigrant families in Leon county.
Caucus "HRC" was started by Caucus President Claudia Sperber, Chaired by Karolyn Burns, and headquartered at
Florida People's Advocacy Center in Tallahassee.  It's a dynamic, direct action committee.
Use the [contact form](contact) if you'd like to help out or learn more!
